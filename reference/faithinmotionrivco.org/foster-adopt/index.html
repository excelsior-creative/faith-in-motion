Working With Faith in Motion

Attend Faith community event about fostering a child in Riverside County. Learn from others about the call to become a foster or adoptive parent.
A Faith in Motion Liaison will contact you to learn more about your family, answer any questions you may have, and discuss the next steps in your journey.
Faith in Motion will get to know you and your family on a deeper level! We ensure that you are a good candidate for fostering or adoption.
Your Faith in Motion Liaison will share with you those agencies that best suit you and your family.
You will now begin your foster journey with your agency! Your Faith in Motion Liason will conduct check-ins and continue to support you throughout the process.
